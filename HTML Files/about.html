Who are we?

We are a team of 3 Members who believe in quality reading and learning experiemces via books and other physical reading material. We created this website inordeer to connect avid readers and admirers of this hobby.

We decided to create this website not only to connect reader across the world but also to provide a common platform for everyone to find whichever book they may require from out databases which keep updating with time, not only availabilty but reviews of various book and materials is also kept in mind for best experiences.
